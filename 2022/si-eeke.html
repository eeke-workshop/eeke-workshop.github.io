Special Issue in Extraction and Evaluation of Knowledge Entities from Scientific Documents


Accepted submissions at 3rd Workshop on Extraction and Evaluation of Knowledge Entities from Scientific Documents (EEKE2022) at the JCDL2022 will be invited to submit extended papers to our special issue in Scientometrics .
The open call for papers at Scientometrics.

Submission Details and Deadlines

  • January 15, 2023: All submissions due
    • Original content typeset abiding by the Scientometrics Authors' Guidelines
    • Submission via the  Scientometrics Editorial Manager under “S.I. : EEKE” 
  • 1st round of reviews
    • March 15, 2023: Referees' reports with decision (accept, minor/major modifications, reject) sent to authors 
    • May 15, 2023: Revised manuscripts due
  • 2nd round of reviews
    • July 15, 2023: Referees' 2nd reports with decision (accept, minor/major modifications, reject) sent to authors
    • September 15, 2023: Revised manuscripts due
  • Online publication with citable DOI in Early View: 3-4 weeks upon acceptance
  • Publication in the Special Issue of Scientometrics: Winter 2023
