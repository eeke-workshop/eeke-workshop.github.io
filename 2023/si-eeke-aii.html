#1: Special Issue in AI + Informetrics


Accepted submissions at Joint Workshop of the 4th Extraction and Evaluation of Knowledge Entities from Scientific Documents (EEKE2023) and the 3rd AI + Informetrics (AII2023) at the JCDL2023 will be invited to submit extended papers to our special issue in Journal of Informetrics.

We also welcome new submission which is not submitted to EEKE-AII2023.

The open Call for Papers can be visite via Journal of Informetrics.

Please note that :

  • If you paper was accepted by EEKE-AII2023, at least 30% new work is required to be added to your paper – e.g., a literature review section is usually required for a journal submission, and improved methods and solid validation measurements could be a bonus.
  • If you paper was accepted by EEKE-AII2023, please acknowledge the early version of your paper presented in the EEKE-AII2023 workshop in the Acknowledgements.
  • A review process following review instruction of Journal of Informetrics will be applied, and we do not guarantee the acceptance of your paper.

Submission Details and Deadlines

  • Deadline for manuscript submission: 1 December 2023
  • Manuscripts should be submitted online at https://www.editorialmanager.com/joi/default2.aspx 
  • Please select “VSI: AI & Informetrics” when submitting your manuscript to this special issue and indicate the actual article type in the cover letter..

Guest Editors

  • Professor Min Song, Yonsei University, Seoul, Korea
  • Professor Chengzhi Zhang, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China.
  • Dr. Yi Zhang, University of Technology Sydney, Sydney, Australia.
